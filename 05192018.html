- 05/19/2018 -

Saturday

I feel kinda shitty today because of yesterday. Yesterday started out like any other day when I'm home alone--I wake up drink coffee and don't eat anything. Yesterday I was also feeling the effects of drinking laxative tea, but it wasn't as satisfying as it normally is? I mean I was able to go and empty my bowels a bit, but I don't feel like I was completely empty? It's hard to explain sometimes when I can't even remember what I was thinking or feeling at the time. It's frustrating that my mind can't seem to recall details and events unless they were somewhat significant to me. But I do remember that yesterday I ate a lot more than I anticipated. Some of it because of my family making me eat, some of it because I just kept on eating. I felt really bad about it. Last night I fell asleep with a full stomach which is one of my pet peeves. I feel that if I sleep after eating that I'll just put on all that food as fat. Last night I didn't sleep very well either. I kept on having nightmares about school and food and waking up in the middle of the night. I didn't get out of bed until 1 PM on Saturday.

I thought that today would have been like a "day of repentance" for yesterday, but my brother came home and made me eat some pizza that he made (He works at Franco's). I also ate a ripe banana and a Clif Bar and I feel like a failure. I tried to purge some of the pizza in the bathroom, but I stopped myself. Bread isn't the nicest of things to purge so I felt like even trying would be in vain.

This evening, I ran an ok amount and then when I got home my mom and dad made me eat dinner with them. The food was good and all, but it just hurts my heart to eat. I even broke my rule of no soda to tried to make it easier for me to purge. After dinner I threw up a bit, but it was not enough to make an impact. I feel like a pig. I keep eating too much and not being able to burn it off. It makes me feel so moody and agitated I don't know what to do. Normally, I only drink laxative tea a couple times a month--but I think this month I would have drank it five to six times already. I know I run the risk of messing up my intestines and my digestive system, but I feel so anxious with all that food inside me I just want it all out as soon as possible. I know that using laxatives won't render the calories meaningless but it's the empty feeling after using laxatives that makes me enjoy using them. So I decided to eat some granola bars in order to get some more fiber in my body so that the laxative tea has something to push out. Often, I keep forgetting to drink my tea while it's warm so that may be one reason why it doesn't work as well.

My parents also wanted to spend some time with me this evening to watch some TV but I just can't keep spending time with them. Personally, I like being alone and just spending time by myself. I love my parents, but if I have to dedicate spending time with them I can't exercise in the privacy in my room or even write in peace. Right now, I'm just relaxing in my room, watching Spongebob, and waiting to drink my lax tea. I may or may not stay up late tonight to exercise. Though I really should, I panic thinking that my arms have gotten bigger. I feel like they jiggle so much more now it makes me so agitated. It might just all be in my head, but I seriously feel like my upper arms have gotten squishier. It makes me feel so bad and self-conscious. I worked really hard to try to make them smaller, I'd be upset if all this eating reversed all my efforts.

I put some thought into it today and I know that I can't keep going on living like this. I mean I can't really picture my life without my eating disorder, but I know there's no way I can live a meaningful life with it. I thought about how nice it'd be not to worry every time I put food in my mouth, but if I stop worrying then a part of me really would be gone. Also, if I stop worrying, then all the years I spent agonizing over food and my body would be for naught. I can't bring myself to stop after investing so much time into it. Sometimes I think that after I reach my goals I'd be able to stop--after all I'm only five pounds away--but I don't know. Of course I want to be healthy and I think that maybe I should just focus my efforts into getting "fit" but I still don't know how I feel about it. Recently I've been lifting weights to help tone up my body, but then that also led to me building some muscle. I don't know how I feel about building muscle. I guess to an extent it's ok to me? Building muscle would require putting on weight though? Honestly, it's not the number that I care so much about, it's how comfortable I feel in my skin at that number? Like if I weighed 120 lbs and felt like the queen of the world then that would be fine with me. But I'm 93 lbs and I feel like a piggy.

I want to say that I'm a rational person and that I know my common sense will kick in when things get bad, but sometimes I have my doubts about that. I feel like my common sense is kicking in now saying that the marginal utility of prolonging my disorded eating is diminishing, but I've grown so accustomed to it I just really can't let it go yet. I don't want to have gotten this close and not have even reached my last goal. I tried explaining to Trey how I want to let all of this go while at the same time not wanting to let it all go and I don't think he completely understands what I mean. It's hard to try to talk to other people about it when no one else can relate. It makes me wish I had a friend who was dealing with the same things as me, but even then I'd question how long that friendship would last.
